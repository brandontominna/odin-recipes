Burger Recipe

Step by step burger recipe

This burger recipe will surely impress your friends, it is simple, easy to make and delicious and takes very little time to prepare

Ingredients
  • ground beef
  • buns
  • ketchup
  • mustard
  • salt
  • pepper
  • onions
  • cheese
  • oil
Steps
  1. Cut onions into small cubes
  2. Carmelize onions in a pan, then move them into a seperate bowl
  3. Form burger patties and put salt and pepper on both sides
  4. Place oil on pan and wait for it to get very hot
  5. Place seasoned patties on skillet, smash them down and wait 3min for a crust to form
  6. Scrape the bottom of the patty then flip it over, place cheese on charred side
  7. Remove patties from skillet
  8. Toast buns
  9. Assemble burger, bottom bun, patty, ketchup and mustard, carmelized onions, top bun
  10. Enjoy
