Burrito Recipe

Step by Step instructions on how to make a Burrito

This burrito recipe takes quite some time to make, but it is worth it in the end and serves 2 people for up to 3 days worth of meals, it is also very delicious and enjouyble to make.

Ingredients
  • 1kg of ground beef
  • garlic powder, onion powder, salt, pepper, cumin, oregano, paprika
  • 3 cans of canned tomatoes
  • beef stock
  • 700 grams of canned beans
  • tomatoes, onions, parsely, lemon
  • tortillas
  • sour cream
Steps
  1. First, prepare the beef mix by sweating 3 large onions with salt to make the process faster
  2. Add ground beef into the large pot and mix it with cumin, paprika, onion and garlic powder, salt and pepper
  3. Wait around 15 minutes for the ground beef to cook
  4. Add about a cup of beef stock and wait until it cooks off
  5. Add the 3 cans of crushed tomatoes and beans, also add more of the seasonings mentioned earlier, wait about 45 minutes for it to cook and all liquids evaporate
  6. Next, prepare the vegtable mix
  7. Cut 3 large tomatoes and 1.5 onions into small cubes and mix them into a bowl
  8. Finely chop parsely, and add it to the bowl along with lemon juice, salt and pepper
  9. Next, prepare the burrito
  10. Take a tortilla and put the meat mixture, vegtable mixture, cheese, and sour cream into it, then fold it into a burrito shape
  11. Lastly, heat up the tortilla on all sides in a pan
  12. Enjoy
