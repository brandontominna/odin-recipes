Pasta Recipe

Step by Step instructions on a pasta recipe

This is a pasta recipe for a dinner for 2, very delicious and easy to prepare

Ingredients
  • pasta
  • salt, oregano, basil, pepper
  • ground beef
  • onions
  • carrots
  • water
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Cream
Steps
  1. First, boil about 4 cups of water in a large pot, add salt into the water for it to boil faster, and for taste.
  2. PLace the pasta and let it cook until ready, should take about 12 minutes, then place it in a seperate container
  3. Dice carrots into small cubes, as well as onions, and put them in a pan with oil and salt, let them get soft which takes about 10 minutes
  4. After the vegtables become soft, add the ground beef along with salt, pepper.
  5. Let the ground beed cook until browned, then add canned tomatoes, wait around 15 minutes for the water from the tomatoes to cook off.
  6. Add the cream and let it cook until mixture becomes creamy and coats a spoon, should not be liquid
  7. ENjoy
